## Description

**Your Apple Watch already tracks your health. Pulse tells you what to do with it.**

Pulse turns raw HealthKit data into a daily recovery score and personalized training plan. No subscription. No cloud uploads. Just smart, local AI that learns your body.

### 🎯 DAILY RECOVERY SCORE
Wake up to a 0-100 score based on your HRV, resting heart rate, sleep quality, and blood oxygen. Know instantly whether today is a push day or a rest day.

Unlike generic thresholds, Pulse builds a personal baseline from YOUR data using statistical analysis. Your "good" is different from everyone else's — Pulse knows that.

### 🏋️ SMART TRAINING PLANS
- Automatic Push/Pull/Legs rotation based on your history
- Progressive overload tracking — know when to add weight
- Intensity adjusted to your recovery score
- Specific exercises with sets, reps, and weight suggestions

### 😴 SLEEP ANALYSIS
- Duration, deep sleep, and REM breakdown
- Sleep regularity tracking
- Impact on next-day performance

### ⚡ ANOMALY DETECTION
Pulse watches for warning signs you might miss:
- HRV dropping for 3+ days → possible overtraining
- Resting heart rate spike → your body needs recovery
- Sleep debt accumulating → automatic rest day suggestion

### ⌚ APPLE WATCH
- Complications showing your daily score at a glance
- Real-time workout tracking with heart rate zones
- Gym arrival detection — auto-suggests your workout
- Haptic feedback for training milestones

### 🤖 AI COACHING (via OpenClaw)
Connect to your own OpenClaw agent for:
- Personalized nutrition timing advice
- Natural language training questions
- Weekly progress reports
- Works without OpenClaw too — core features are fully local

### 🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
- All data stays on YOUR device
- No accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking
- No ads, ever
- Open about what we collect: nothing
