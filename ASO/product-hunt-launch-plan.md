### Maker Comment（发布后立刻发）
Hey PH! 👋

I'm Khalil, and I built Pulse because I was tired of paying $30/month for WHOOP just to see a recovery score.

Your Apple Watch already has the sensors — HRV, heart rate, sleep, blood oxygen. Pulse turns that raw data into:

🎯 Daily 0-100 recovery score (personalized to YOUR baseline, not generic thresholds)
🏋️ Smart training plans that adapt to how you feel
⚡ Anomaly alerts when your body needs rest
🤖 Optional AI coaching via OpenClaw

The kicker? **$4.99 once. Forever.** No subscription. All data stays on your device.

Built with SwiftUI, HealthKit, and a lot of late nights. Would love your feedback!
