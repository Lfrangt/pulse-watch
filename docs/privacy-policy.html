Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

🔒 Pulse Watch is privacy-first. All your health data stays on your device. We never collect, store, or transmit your personal health information to any server.

Data We Access

Health Data (HealthKit)

Pulse Watch reads the following from Apple HealthKit:

  • Heart rate, resting heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Blood oxygen saturation (SpO2)
  • Sleep analysis (duration, stages)
  • Step count
  • Active and resting energy burned

The App writes workout data to HealthKit when you complete a workout on Apple Watch.

All HealthKit data is processed and stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It is never transmitted to external servers.

Location Data

The App uses location services to detect when you arrive at a saved gym location for automatic training suggestions. Location data is processed entirely on-device, never uploaded to any server, and never shared with third parties.

Camera

The App uses the camera solely to scan QR codes for optional OpenClaw pairing. No images or video are stored or transmitted.

Analytics

The App uses TelemetryDeck for anonymous, privacy-preserving usage analytics. TelemetryDeck does not collect personal information and complies with GDPR. No health data is included in analytics.

Optional: OpenClaw Integration

If you choose to connect to your own OpenClaw instance (an optional AI coaching feature), the App transmits aggregated health summaries to your specified server. This connection is:

  • Entirely opt-in
  • Configured by you with your own server URL and credentials
  • Secured with bearer token authentication over HTTPS
  • Disconnectable at any time

We do not operate or have access to your OpenClaw server.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Health records: SwiftData (on-device database)
  • Preferences: UserDefaults (on-device)
  • OpenClaw token: iOS Keychain (encrypted, on-device)
  • Widget data: App Group container (on-device)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third party.

Children's Privacy

Pulse Watch is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally, you have full control:

  • Access: View all your data in the App at any time
  • Deletion: Delete the App to remove all data, or clear data from Settings
  • Portability: Your health data remains in Apple HealthKit

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at abundra.dev@gmail.com or via GitHub Issues.

隐私政策

最后更新：2026年3月16日

🔒 Pulse Watch 隐私优先。您的所有健康数据均存储在您的设备上。我们绝不收集、存储或传输您的个人健康信息到任何服务器。

我们访问的数据

健康数据 (HealthKit)

Pulse Watch 从 Apple HealthKit 读取以下数据：

  • 心率、静息心率、心率变异性 (HRV)
  • 血氧饱和度 (SpO2)
  • 睡眠分析（时长、阶段）
  • 步数
  • 活动与静息能量消耗

当您在 Apple Watch 上完成训练时，本应用会将运动数据写入 HealthKit。

所有 HealthKit 数据均使用 Apple 的 SwiftData 框架在设备本地处理和存储，绝不会传输到外部服务器。

位置数据

本应用使用位置服务检测您是否到达已保存的健身房位置，以提供自动训练建议。位置数据完全在设备上处理，绝不上传到任何服务器，也绝不与第三方共享。

相机

本应用仅使用相机扫描 OpenClaw 配对二维码。不存储或传输任何图像或视频。

分析

本应用使用 TelemetryDeck 进行匿名、隐私保护的使用分析。TelemetryDeck 不收集个人信息，符合 GDPR 规定。分析数据中不包含任何健康数据。

可选：OpenClaw 集成

如果您选择将本应用连接到您自己的 OpenClaw 实例（可选的 AI 教练功能），本应用会将聚合的健康摘要传输到您指定的服务器。此连接：

  • 完全由用户选择启用
  • 由您使用自己的服务器 URL 和凭据配置
  • 通过 HTTPS 的 Bearer Token 认证保护
  • 您可以随时断开连接

我们不运营也无法访问您的 OpenClaw 服务器。

数据存储

所有数据存储在设备本地：

  • 健康记录：SwiftData（设备数据库）
  • 偏好设置：UserDefaults（设备本地）
  • OpenClaw 令牌：iOS 钥匙串（加密存储）
  • 小组件数据：App Group 容器（设备本地）

数据共享

我们不会向任何第三方出售、共享或传输您的个人数据。

儿童隐私

Pulse Watch 不面向 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会故意收集儿童数据。

您的权利

由于所有数据存储在本地，您拥有完全控制权：

  • 访问：随时在应用中查看所有数据
  • 删除：删除应用即可移除所有数据，或在设置中清除
  • 可携带：您的健康数据保留在 Apple HealthKit 中

政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。变更将在上方"最后更新"日期中体现。
