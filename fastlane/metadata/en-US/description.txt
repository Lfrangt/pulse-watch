Stop deciphering dashboards. Pulse reads your Apple Watch health data and gives you one number: your daily health score.

Heart rate, HRV, sleep, steps, blood oxygen — collected automatically, scored from 0 to 100. Open the app, see your score, know how your body is doing. That's it.

WHAT YOU GET:

• Daily Health Score — sleep, HRV, resting heart rate, and activity distilled into a single actionable number
• Morning Brief — wake up to a personalized health summary notification
• Real-time workout tracking — heart rate zones on your Apple Watch, live during every session
• 7-day trend charts — heart rate, HRV, and sleep trends so you can spot patterns
• Training history — every workout saved with duration, heart rate zones, and calories
• Heart rate alerts — get notified when your resting heart rate is unusually high or low
• Gym detection — arrive at the gym and get automatic PPL training suggestions
• Widgets everywhere — Home Screen, Lock Screen, and Apple Watch complications
• Share your wins — generate workout achievement cards for Instagram, Twitter, or group chats

WHY PULSE:

No subscription. Buy once, own forever. Your health data never leaves your device — no accounts, no cloud uploads, no tracking. Just you and your data.

Most health apps bury you in charts. Pulse gives you one score and tells you what it means. Less time analyzing, more time living.

OPTIONAL AI COACHING:

Connect your own OpenClaw instance for AI-powered training advice, anomaly alerts, and conversational health coaching. Your data stays on your server, not ours.

REQUIREMENTS:

• iPhone with iOS 17.0+
• Apple Watch with watchOS 10.0+ (recommended for full features)
• HealthKit data from Apple Watch or compatible sensors
