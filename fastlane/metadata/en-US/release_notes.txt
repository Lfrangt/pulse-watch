Welcome to Pulse 1.0 — your AI fitness coach for Apple Watch.

What's in this release:

• Daily Coach — Score-banded actionable advice on the dashboard, citing your sleep, RHR, and HRV. Tap "Today's Coach" to see what your body needs.
• AI Coach (DeepSeek) — Optional cloud AI for personalized training advice. One-tap enable in Settings with full data-flow disclosure.
• Apple Watch confidence indicator — Hero card shows data source + completeness so you know how trusted today's score is.
• Daily readiness score (0–100) — Combined recovery from sleep, HRV, RHR, and activity.
• Strength training — Squat / bench / deadlift with auto-PR detection and bodyweight-ratio level assessment.
• Health Age — Biological age estimated from 5 metrics.
• Sleep, Activity, Strain & Recovery scores — Independent scores with tap-to-expand factor breakdown.
• Weekly trends — 7-day rolling charts for HR, HRV, sleep with interactive drill-in.
• Morning Brief notification — Score + sleep + training advice every morning.
• Streak system — Daily tracking streak with milestones at 7 / 30 / 100 / 365 days.
• Apple Watch complications, Home Screen + Lock Screen widgets.
• Manual workout entry, gym arrival auto-detection (PPL rotation suggestions).
• Privacy first — All health data on-device by default. AI Coach is opt-in.
