暖色调，有质感，不要AI味 — warm earthy palette (golds, ambers, sage greens, terracotta), soft shadows over hard borders, spring animations, staggered card entrances, breathing background gradients.
**Why:** User explicitly wants the app to feel like a thoughtfully designed indie product, not a corporate or AI-generated template.
**How to apply:** Use PulseTheme colors consistently. Prefer `.continuous` corner style. Use `.pulseCard()` modifier for cards. No blue/purple tech colors. No glassmorphism.
